about chrisChris Ruggiero is a film composer and music producer. Recent film scores include Hooligan Sparrow (Sundance Film Festival 2016, currently in theatrical release), Indian Point (Tribeca Film Festival 2015, currently in theatrical release), and Kehinde Wiley: An Economy of Grace (PBS), winner of the SXSW Grand Jury Prize for Best Documentary Short. His original compositions currently appear in over 100 television series including The Voice, Pawn Stars, and American Pickers. Commercial credits include music for brands such as Coke, HSN, Imodium, Dewars, and Liberty Mutual. Upcoming film scores include the documentaries What Was Ours (ITVS) and Out Run. He records and produces bands and artists from his studio Gold Coast Recorders, lectures on film, sound, and semiotics at the University of Bridgeport, and runs the popular audio-history website Preservation Sound.
